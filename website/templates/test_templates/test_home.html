Welcome To Melometrics! (Test Version)

Melometrics is a application designed to enhance your spotify experience. We currently have 3 features up and running.

Liked Song Analysis

Do you find that you liked songs are unorganised and hard to search through? Well click here to see your liked songs ranked by 7 different metrics. These include BPM, energy, positivity, instrumentalness and more! This tool is perfect for satisfying you own curiosity or as an assistant when tryig to create new playlists!

Liked Songs

Top Tracks

Do you like knowing what your gmost played songs are but dont want to wait all year for your Spotify wrapped? Well click here to see your top 100 tracks over 3 different time scales! We even have the option of turning these tracks into a playlist for you!

Top Artists

Ever wondered who your most listened to artists are? Ever wondered who you'e been listening to recently, compared to who you've been listening to consistently for a while? Well click here to find out who you top artists are over 3 different lengths of time?
